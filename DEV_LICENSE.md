**3.1.** Open Source Assets published in the Public Repository are governed by the APL-SA license. In the event of conflict between APL-SA terms and this Agreement, APL-SA takes precedence for those asset types.

**3.2.** Unbinarised .p3d source files held in the Private Repository are not open source and are not subject to APL-SA. Access is granted solely at the Maintainer's discretion and governed by this Agreement.

**3.3.** The Maintainer is responsible for combining Open Source Assets and Private Repository assets to produce the Steam Workshop release. This build process does not alter the ownership or license terms of any individual Asset.

**3.4.** GitHub releases are partial releases and do not constitute full distribution of the Mod. End users seeking the full Mod are directed to the Steam Workshop.

## 4. Maintainer Succession

**4.1.** If the current Maintainer is permanently stepping down from the project, they must nominate a successor Maintainer in writing prior to their departure.

**4.2.** Upon nomination and acceptance by the successor, all rights held by the outgoing Maintainer under this Agreement transfer in full to the incoming Maintainer.

**4.3.** If a Maintainer becomes permanently unreachable without nominating a successor, the active Development Team may collectively appoint a successor by majority agreement, documented in writing.
