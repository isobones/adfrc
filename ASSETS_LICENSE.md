# Asset Licensing

This document covers the licensing of 3D models (`.p3d` files) and other binary assets in ADF: Re-Cut. For the legal agreement governing the development team, see [DEV_LICENSE.md](DEV_LICENSE.md). For code, configs, scripts, and textures, see [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md).

---

## Overview

Models in ADF: Re-Cut fall into three categories:

1. **Protected legacy models** — Made by the original team, restricted licensing
2. **Open-source models from other APL-SA mods** — Used under APL-SA per their original terms
3. **Open-source models contributed under APL-SA** — Either self-released by the team or contributed by the community

Categories 2 and 3 are present in this public repository. Category 1 is not — those models exist only in the team's private repository and are distributed solely as binarised files via the Steam Workshop release.

For attribution of specific models, see [MODEL_CREDITS.md](MODEL_CREDITS.md).

These models are protected by copyright. Unauthorised use is prohibited. The restriction exists to protect the work of developers who contributed to the project and may no longer be active.

A protected legacy model can only be relicensed (e.g. moved to APL-SA) with the original creator's explicit written agreement, consistent with clauses 1.1 and 2.2 of [DEV_LICENSE.md](DEV_LICENSE.md).

## 2. Open-Source Models from Other Mods

Some models in this repository originate from other APL-SA Arma 3 mods and are used under the terms of that license. These are credited in [MODEL_CREDITS.md](MODEL_CREDITS.md) along with their source mod and original author.

Use of these models within ADF: Re-Cut complies with APL-SA. Anyone forking or building upon ADF: Re-Cut must continue to credit the original authors and remain under APL-SA terms.

---

## 3. Open-Source Models Contributed Under APL-SA

This category covers:

- Models the development team has elected to release under APL-SA
- New models contributed to the project by the community after open-sourcing

By contributing a new `.p3d` to this repository, the contributor agrees that:

- Their contribution is licensed under APL-SA
- The model may be used, modified, and distributed as part of this mod
- They retain copyright but grant the community rights per APL-SA terms
- The model will be distributed in binarised form as part of the Steam Workshop release
- They are responsible for binarising their own model before submission, or coordinating with the maintainer

If you want to contribute a model but retain stricter licensing, contact @IsoBones on the [Discord](https://discord.gg/sNKr7fwHUr) before submitting.

All open-source models — both pre-existing and newly contributed — should be credited in [MODEL_CREDITS.md](MODEL_CREDITS.md).

## Distribution and End-User Rights

The Steam Workshop release combines all three categories into a single binarised distribution. End users have the right to use the released mod for personal, non-commercial purposes — gameplay, mission making, and server hosting. End users do not have the right to extract, repack, or redistribute any models from the binarised release, regardless of the underlying licensing of individual files.

For developers wanting to use APL-SA-licensed models from this mod in their own project: clone this repository for the source files. Do not extract from the binarised Workshop release.
